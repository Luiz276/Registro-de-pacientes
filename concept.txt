Program that registers patients of a hospital.
To each patient is given a number(code) according to arrival order.
They are cataloged in the following manner: numeric code, name, time of arrival, hospital wing, condition(in treatment or discharged).

Possible operations:
0-close(all data is saved to a txt file)
1-new patient
2-serach patient
3-alter patient data
4-discharge a patient
5-list all patients
